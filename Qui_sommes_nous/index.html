.Who am I

This is the page where you can write anything that you like about you.

About Me pages are often very personal and I'm just providing you with an idea for this page, you probably want to create one that makes you happy. Unfortunately, that means that you need to do the editing of this page on your own, if this is a daunting task you could simply edit this text and the skills text in the code of the about.html page.

This theme was created by hand from scratch using bootstrap. The Sass files were taken from the minima theme, but 98% of it was modified by me. This was my first serious attempt at crafting a theme out of nothing, I hope you like it and please do let me know your opinion about it.
